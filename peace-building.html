Peace Building

My Peace Building Journey

As a certified peace builder and refugee from South Sudan, I have dedicated myself to fostering peace, reconciliation, and conflict resolution in displaced communities. My work focuses on building bridges between refugee and host communities, promoting dialogue, and developing sustainable peace initiatives.

Core Peace Building Principles

  • Inclusive Dialogue: Creating safe spaces for open communication between different community groups
  • Conflict Resolution: Implementing mediation and negotiation techniques to resolve disputes
  • Community Reconciliation: Facilitating healing and reconciliation processes
  • Capacity Building: Training community members in peace building skills
  • Sustainable Peace: Developing long-term strategies for maintaining peace

ACCORD Certification

Certified peace builder trained by the African Centre for the Constructive Resolution of Disputes (ACCORD) in conflict management and resolution, mediation, and dialogue facilitation.

IPHRD-Africa Training

Trained by International Center for Peace, Human Rights and Development (IPHRD-Africa) in advanced peace building methodologies and human rights-based approaches to conflict resolution.

Kuza Amani Fellowship

Alumni of the Kuza Amani Fellowship, a prestigious program that equips peace builders with innovative approaches to community-based conflict transformation.

Youth in Action Fellowship

Graduate of the Youth in Action Fellowship, focusing on youth-led peace building initiatives and community mobilization for sustainable peace.

Peace Building Programs and Initiatives

Community Dialogue Forums

Established regular community dialogue forums that bring together refugee and host community members to discuss issues, share perspectives, and work towards mutual understanding. These forums have become platforms for addressing tensions and building relationships.

Conflict Mediation Services

Provided mediation services for conflicts within refugee communities and between refugee and host communities. This includes family disputes, resource conflicts, and inter-community tensions.

Peace Education Programs

Developed and implemented peace education programs in schools and community centers, teaching conflict resolution skills, tolerance, and peaceful coexistence to children and youth.

Reconciliation Workshops

Conducted reconciliation workshops that help communities heal from past conflicts and trauma, using culturally appropriate methods and traditional reconciliation practices.

Youth Peace Ambassadors

Trained and mentored youth peace ambassadors who serve as role models and peace advocates within their communities, spreading messages of tolerance and non-violence.

Inter-Community Projects

Facilitated collaborative projects between refugee and host communities, such as shared farming initiatives, cultural exchange programs, and joint community development activities.

Impact and Achievements

Through my peace building work, I have successfully facilitated numerous conflict resolutions and contributed to improved relations between refugee and host communities. Key achievements include:

  • Resolved over 50 community conflicts through mediation and dialogue
  • Trained 200+ community members in peace building and conflict resolution skills
  • Established 10 community dialogue forums across refugee settlements
  • Mentored 25 youth peace ambassadors who continue to promote peace in their communities
  • Facilitated reconciliation processes for communities affected by inter-ethnic tensions

Recognition and Representation

In 2023, I was selected to represent refugee youth by the United Nations Human Rights Office of the High Commissioner for East and Horn of Africa in a consultation meeting on fostering youth participation in governance, peace and security in the context of human rights 75. This recognition highlights the importance of including refugee voices in peace building and governance processes.

Future Vision

My vision is to expand peace building initiatives to reach more refugee communities across Uganda and the region, while developing innovative approaches that address the unique challenges faced by displaced populations. I believe that sustainable peace can only be achieved through inclusive, community-driven processes that respect the dignity and rights of all individuals.
