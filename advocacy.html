My Advocacy Journey

As a refugee rights advocate with five years of experience in displaced settings, I have dedicated myself to amplifying the voices of refugee communities and advocating for their rights, dignity, and inclusion. My advocacy work spans multiple areas including refugee rights, sexual reproductive health and rights, and peace building.

Advocacy Approach

  • Community-Centered: Ensuring that advocacy efforts are driven by the needs and voices of refugee communities
  • Evidence-Based: Using research and data to inform advocacy strategies and policy recommendations
  • Collaborative: Working with diverse stakeholders including government, NGOs, and international organizations
  • Sustainable: Building long-term advocacy capacity within communities
  • Inclusive: Ensuring that marginalized voices, especially women and youth, are represented

Recent Advocacy Campaigns and Initiatives (2024-2025)

Refugee Household Prioritization (Ration Cuts) Advocacy Project

As co-founder at Youth Action for Community Transformation (YACOT), I implemented a critical advocacy project in the Rhino Camp Refugee Settlement, focusing on addressing the devastating effects of refugee household prioritization (ration cuts) introduced in July 2023. The project directly impacted 691 refugees, including 324 individuals aged 15–35 years and 367 aged 36+ years, comprising 468 females, 193 males, and 30 persons with disabilities.

Through strategic engagement, we indirectly reached an estimated 3,000+ refugees and host community members across the West Nile Sub-Region. This outreach was amplified by two radio talk shows at Arua One FM, which broadcast to eight districts, and six community dialogues involving refugees and key stakeholders.

The activities provided a platform for raising awareness, fostering dialogue, and identifying coping mechanisms for the challenges posed by ration cuts. Debates, dramas, and dialogues highlighted the lived experiences of refugees and encouraged collaborative solutions.

Refugee Rights Awareness Campaign

Led comprehensive awareness campaigns on refugee rights, including access to education, healthcare, and legal protection. These campaigns reached thousands of refugees and host community members, increasing understanding of refugee rights and responsibilities.

Youth Participation in Governance

Advocated for increased youth participation in governance and decision-making processes affecting refugee communities. This included organizing youth forums, training sessions, and policy dialogues with government officials and international organizations.

Access to Education for Refugee Children

Campaigned for improved access to quality education for refugee children, addressing barriers such as language, cultural differences, and resource constraints. This advocacy led to increased enrollment and better educational support.

Healthcare Access Advocacy

Advocated for improved healthcare access for refugees, particularly focusing on maternal and child health, mental health services, and specialized care for vulnerable populations.

Gender Equality and Women's Rights

Led advocacy efforts to promote gender equality and women's rights within refugee communities, addressing issues such as gender-based violence, women's participation in decision-making, and access to reproductive health services.

Policy Advocacy and Stakeholder Engagement

My advocacy work extends beyond community-level initiatives to include engagement with policymakers, government officials, and international organizations. I have participated in numerous policy dialogues and consultation meetings to ensure that refugee voices are heard in decision-making processes.

Future Advocacy Goals

My future advocacy goals include expanding the reach of refugee rights advocacy to more communities, developing innovative advocacy strategies using technology and social media, and building stronger networks of refugee advocates across the region. I believe that sustainable change comes from empowering communities to advocate for themselves while providing the necessary support and resources.
